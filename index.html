What is Collabrain?

Collabrain is a platform made for you to reduce the barries to skill development: Technical, vocational, education and training (TVET), from secondary to tertiary education.
Providing lifelong learning opportunities for youth and adults will lay the foundation for solving other problems plaguing the world.

Why Collabrain?

With Collabrain, you will:

Learn New Knowledge

This website lets you search for people who are willing to share their knowledge (teach you) about the topics you are interested in learning for FREE!

Share What You Know

You will also be able to tutor people in the areas you are already capable of teaching. We believe everyone has an inner teacher who is ready to come out and share themselves to the world!

Help make a better World

In many parts of the world education is a struggle for people. This plataform has a goal to close this gap. Be part of the solution!!

Meet Our CollabrainTeam
